Narberth

The Valleys

A town where love and support flows in abundance

So much hope driven from the elders to the youngsters

The holding cell holds on to passion so well

With Narbeth youth it’s a group I instantly connected to

From the facilitators to the facilitated

Everybody’s connected everyone’s respected

From the bar to raising the bar

Development is never too far

It’s almost at arms reach

One body of people with a special heartbeat

These connections are special

So surreal 3 days have been epic

Appreciate what you have and cherish it

I’ll cherish the memories and hopefully we’ll make many more

I’m going to miss the

The Friendships the families, the happiness and respect

I almost love the valleys and you guys are the best.

Ali Zay
